About Wafl Bar

Founded with a passion for authentic, handcrafted treats, Wafl Bar brings together the best of Belgian waffle tradition and Italian pizzelle artistry. Every product is made in small batches using premium ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Our waffles are crafted with organic flour, real vanilla, and pure butter, then flash-frozen to preserve their fresh-baked taste. Our pizzelles follow authentic Italian family recipes, pressed to delicate perfection with just the right amount of sweetness.

From our kitchen to your table, we're committed to delivering exceptional quality and unforgettable flavors that bring joy to every bite.
